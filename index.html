The Challenge

Cloud AI Creates a Security Trade-Off

The productivity gains from mainstream AI have come at the expense of privacy. Sending sensitive corporate data to third-party clouds introduces significant risks: data harvesting, interception, rising costs, and a complete loss of data sovereignty. This is a critical barrier for regulated industries.
